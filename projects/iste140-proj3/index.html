Introduction

Many forms of media in this digital age affects how we, our children, and our friends see ourselves. Some of it can be positive, but a lot more of it is negatively affecting our daily lives transparently. In the articles below, you can find out how visual culture, social media, and the internet all intertwine to set standards for our society.

Female Representation in Visual Culture and How it Affects Society

Media is woven into our daily lives and subconsciously affects how we view men and women. All forms of media communicate images of the different genders, many of which insinuate unrealistic, stereotypical, and limiting perceptions. These underlying themes in the media we consume normalize traditional roles in relationships and inequality for women in most aspects of life.
